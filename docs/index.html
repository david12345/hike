What's inside

📍

GPS Tracking

Record your route with high-accuracy GPS. See real-time distance, elapsed time, altitude, and point count as you hike.

🗺

Live Map

Watch your position update on an OpenStreetMap, OpenTopoMap, or satellite imagery base layer. Route polyline drawn in real time.

📶

Offline Tiles

Map tiles are cached to disk for 30 days so you can follow your route without a mobile data connection.

🧭

Compass

Live compass rose with degree readout at the center. Knows your cardinal direction at a glance.

⛅

Weather

Current temperature, weather description, and air pressure pulled from the Open-Meteo API — no API key needed.

📂

Trail Import

Import GPX or KML trails from any source. View them on the map, export them back, or follow them on a guided hike.

📜

Hike Log

Every completed hike is saved locally. Tap any entry to replay the full route on an interactive detail map.

🔋

Battery Friendly

Single GPS stream with tiered accuracy — high while recording, ambient while just browsing. Background service keeps tracking even when the screen is off.

🛰️

Satellite View

Switch any map to satellite imagery (Esri World Imagery) with one tap. Cycle between standard, topographic, and satellite layers on every map screen.

🚗

Android Auto

When Android Auto is connected, Hike shows a live map, compass heading, latitude, longitude, and altitude on your car's display — no phone required.

📐

Accurate Path Recording

Heading-change-triggered GPS sampling faithfully captures roundabouts and switchbacks — not just straight-line displacement. Tighter 1 m / 2 s sampling for denser curves.

📊

Hike Analytics

The Stats tab shows charts and metrics across all your recorded hikes: distance over time, hikes per month, distance distribution, personal bests, and active-day streaks — all filterable by date range.

↕️

Sort & Filter

Sort your hike log by date (newest or oldest first) and your imported trails by name (A → Z or Z → A). Preferences are remembered across sessions.

🌍

Portuguese & English

The app automatically uses Portuguese (pt) or English based on your device language — no setting required. Dates, numbers, and charts adapt to your locale too.

⏸️

Pause & Resume

Take a break without stopping your hike. The Pause button freezes the timer and GPS collection; Resume picks up exactly where you left off. The foreground notification shows "Paused" while you rest.

🎯

GPS Drift Filter

Standing still no longer inflates your distance. A sliding-window centroid filter suppresses jitter fixes while you are stationary, keeping your route line clean and your distance accurate.

How it works

Install the APK

Download the APK and allow installation from unknown sources in your Android settings (Settings → Security → Install unknown apps).

Grant location permission

Hike asks for location permission on first launch. Choose "Allow all the time" to enable background tracking while the screen is off.

Head outside and hit Record

Tap the record button on the Track tab. Your route is saved automatically with checkpoint saves every 30 seconds — so nothing is lost even if the app crashes.

Review your hikes

Open the Log tab to see all past hikes. Tap any entry to view the route on a full-screen map with statistics.

Explore your progress

Switch to the Stats tab for charts and metrics across all your hikes. Filter by date range to see a specific period, track personal bests, and check your active-day streak.

Free & Open Source

Built with Flutter, no subscriptions, no tracking

Hike is free, has no ads, requires no account, and collects no personal data. All hike records are stored only on your device. The source code is available on GitHub.
